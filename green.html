Parker Probes

The first spacecraft to “touch” the sun. Parker Solar has successfully flown through the solar corona, or upper atmosphere, to obtain Particle samples and magnetic fields of the star. The Parker Solar spacecraft was launched in 2018 and started circling closer and closer to the sun. Scientists, including the astrophysicist honored by the name of the spaceship, want to answer questions fundamentals about the solar wind emanating from the sun, flinging energetic particles throughout the solar system.
